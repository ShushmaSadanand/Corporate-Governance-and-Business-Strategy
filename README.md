# Corporate Governance & Business Strategy Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio of management frameworks, strategic analyses, and regulatory compliance case studies developed during my Master's in Management. This space demonstrates a business-oriented approach to corporate sustainability, risk mitigation, and consumer ethics.

---

## Project 1: Sustainable Compliance & Anti-Corruption Training
**File:** `G01_T05_Training.pdf`

A strategic corporate compliance program layout inspired by the AFA (Agence Française Anticorruption) standards, designed to protect organizational prosperity and minimize regulatory risks.

### Core Business Objectives
* **Risk Identification:** Highlighting vulnerabilities across exposed tiers of management regarding compliance, illicit solicitation, and bribery.
* **Corporate Vigilance:** Creating a blueprint for an internal whistleblowing system and continuous monitoring structures to foster shared corporate accountability.
* **Strategic Training:** Establishing targeted training frameworks to mitigate operations-level risk, preventing systemic penalties that averages 2-5% of global GDP.

### Key Competencies Demonstrated
* Regulatory Compliance Strategy (Anti-Corruption)
* Risk Assessment & Mitigation Frameworks
* Leadership & Stakeholder Awareness Design

## Project 2: Greenwashing — Market Analysis & Corporate Impacts
**File:** `G1_T5_GreenWashing.pdf`

A deep-dive research study evaluating the commercial benefits and operational pitfalls of "greenwashing" from a corporate and consumer perspective, utilizing contemporary case frameworks like France’s AGEC and Climate/Resilience laws.

### Core Business Insights
* **Short-term vs. Long-term Value:** Analyzing how superficial environmental claims boost brand valuation and market share temporarily but result in devastating consumer trust degradation and regulatory penalties long-term.
* **Policy Compliance Mapping:** Navigating complex modern circular economy regulations (e.g., plastic phase-outs and advertisement bans) to keep business operations strategy compliant.

### Key Competencies Demonstrated
* Market Research & Consumer Behavior Analysis
* Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) Strategy
* Legal & Policy Impact Mapping
